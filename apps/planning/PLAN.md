# Tangram Puzzle Game

## Project

A tangram is an ancient Chinese dissection puzzle consisting of a large square divided into 7 specific geometric pieces called "tans." 

The set includes:

* 2 large right isosceles triangles
* 1 medium right isosceles triangle
* 2 small right isosceles triangles
* 1 small square1 parallelogram

The objective is to arrange all seven of these pieces—without overlapping—to form various silhouettes, such as animals, humans, letters, or geometric shapes.

## Packs of puzzles

Names of the collections: "Alphabet", "Numbers", "Animals", "Birds", "Boats", "Buildings", "Faces", "Fishes", "Human", "Nature", "Objects", "Shapes", "Sports", "Technical", "Toys", "Cats", "Dogs"
