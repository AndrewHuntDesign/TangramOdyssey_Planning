Puzzling Tangrams is a fun tangram puzzle iPhone application for all ages! Challenge and increase your mental agility by solving tangrams!

A tangram (Chinese: literally "seven boards of skill") is a puzzle consisting of seven flat shapes which when combined, form a specific shape. No pieces may overlap.

This iPhone application will include 

* Over 300 puzzles, with multiple levels of difficulty per puzzle

* Category of puzzles include: animals, letters, numbers, people, and more
* Apple Game Center integration
* Facebook interaction
* Submit your fastest solving time to online scoreboard
* Create your own puzzles and share with others
* Variety of game background screens
* Variety of board piece styles
